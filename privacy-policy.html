Privacy Policy

At Al Aqsa Clinics we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with privacy laws and regulations.

Collection of Personal Information:

We may collect personal information from participants in the medical center campaign, which may include but is not limited to:

  • Contact information (name, address, email, phone number)
  • Demographic information
  • Medical information relevant to the campaign

Use of Personal Information:

The personal information collected will be used for the following purposes:

  • Communication with participants regarding the campaign
  • Customized offers and services related to the campaign
  • Improvement of our services and campaigns

Protection of Personal Information:

We take appropriate measures to protect the personal information collected during the campaign, including but not limited to:

    Sharing of Personal Information:

    We may share personal information with third-party service providers involved in the campaign, strictly for campaign-related purposes. These service providers are obliged to maintain the confidentiality and security of the information.

    Rights of Participants:

    Participants have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of their personal information. They can also opt-out of receiving further communications related to the campaign.

    Please note that this privacy policy may be subject to change, and any updates will be communicated to the participants. By participating in the medical center campaign, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
